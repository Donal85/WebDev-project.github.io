Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Pastry Paradise, your go-to destination for exquisite cakes and delightful treats. By placing an order with us, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

Ordering and Payments

1. All orders must be placed through our website or in-person at our store. We do not accept orders through social media or phone calls.

2. Payment must be made in full at the time of placing the order. We accept online payments through secure channels.

3. Custom orders require a minimum of 3 days' notice. Changes to orders are subject to approval and may incur additional charges.

Delivery and Pickup

1. We offer delivery services within a specified radius. Delivery fees may apply based on the delivery location.

2. Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery details. We are not liable for delays or failed deliveries due to inaccurate information.

3. Pickup orders must be collected at the agreed-upon time. Cakes left unclaimed for more than 24 hours may be discarded, and no refunds will be provided.

Quality and Satisfaction

1. We take pride in crafting high-quality cakes. If you are dissatisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours, and we will address your concerns promptly.

2. We reserve the right to make minor design adjustments to meet quality standards without prior notice.

Cancellations and Refunds

1. Cancellations are accepted 3 days before the scheduled pickup or delivery date. A cancellation fee may apply.

2. No refunds will be provided for cancellations made within 2 days of the scheduled date.

Allergies and Dietary Restrictions

1. While we strive to accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions, our kitchen handles various ingredients, and we cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment.

2. Customers with severe allergies are advised to exercise caution and contact us for detailed ingredient information.

Privacy Policy

1. We respect your privacy. Personal information provided during the ordering process will be securely stored and used solely for order fulfillment purposes.

2. We do not share customer information with third parties without explicit consent.

Changes to Terms and Conditions

We reserve the right to update and modify these terms and conditions at any time. Customers will be notified of significant changes.

Thank you for choosing Pastry Paradise. We look forward to sweetening your special moments with our delectable creations!
